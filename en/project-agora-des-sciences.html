Project

Agora des Sciences

February - July 2026
Team ManagementProject Management

Organizing a one-day event to bring together people involved in science communication and outreach, with the aim of reflecting on and discussing ways to share knowledge by exchanging ideas and perspectives.

Context & Objectives

After several years of setting up science outreach projects and engaging with numerous members of the science communication community, we’ve come to a simple conclusion: we lack a space for professionals in science outreach and communication to meet and exchange ideas. We have therefore decided to launch the Agora des sciences: a day of reflection and discussion on knowledge sharing.

Role & Responsibilities

⚙️ Pre-event planning
  • Contacting speakers
  • Organizing the volunteer schedule
  • External communications (Social media, emails, website setup)
  • Catering management
  • Support for speakers
💬 On-the-day coordination
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Facilitating a roundtable discussion
✍️ Post-event follow-up
  • Setting up an evaluation survey
  • Writing an event report
  • External communication
